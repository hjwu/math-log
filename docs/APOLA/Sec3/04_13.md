## 題目

If $\bm{A}$ is invertible, then ${(\bm{A}^*)}^{-1} = {(\bm{A}^{-1})}^{*}.$

## 解答

$$
\begin{align*}
{(\bm{A}^*)}^{-1} &= (\bm{A}\bm{A}^{-1}){(\bm{A}^*)}^{-1}\\
&= (\bm{A}\bm{A}^{-1})^*{(\bm{A}^*)}^{-1}\\
&= {(\bm{A}^{-1})}^* \bm{A}^* {(\bm{A}^*)}^{-1}\\
&= {(\bm{A}^{-1})}^*
\end{align*}
$$
