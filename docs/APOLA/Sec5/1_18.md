## 題目

If $\bm{A}$ and $\bm{B}$ are $3\times3$ matrices, and $\bm{B}$ is invertible, show that $\det(\bm{A}+x\bm{B}) = 0$ for some $x\in F$.

## 解答

Choose $x$ is an eigenvalue of $\bm{B^{-1}A}$, then $\det(\bm{B})\det(\bm{B^{-1}A}+x\bm{I}) = \det(\bm{A}+x\bm{B}) = 0$
