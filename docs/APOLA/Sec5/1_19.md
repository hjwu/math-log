## 題目

If $\bm{A}$ and $\bm{B}$ are $3\times3$ matrices, and $\bm{B}\neq\bm{O}$ is not invertible. Is there an $x\in F$ s.t. $\det(\bm{A}+x\bm{B}) = 0$ ?

## 解答

Consider $\bm{A}=\bm{I}$ and $\bm{B}=\bm{E}_{1,3}$, then $\det(\bm{A}+x\bm{B}) = 1, \forall x\in F.$
