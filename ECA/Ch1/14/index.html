題目​

  • (a) Prove the Lagrange's identity

    (∑i=1nxiyi)2=(∑i=1nxi2)(∑i=1nyi2)−∑1≤i<j≤n(xiyj−xjyi)2\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}x_iy_i\bigg)^2 = \bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2\bigg)\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2\bigg)-\sum_{1\leq i < j\leq n}(x_iy_j-x_jy_i)^2(i=1∑n​xi​yi​)2=(i=1∑n​xi2​)(i=1∑n​yi2​)−1≤i<j≤n∑​(xi​yj​−xj​yi​)2

    and use this to give another proof of the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality.

  • (b) Show that

    (∑i=1n(xi+yi)2)12≤(∑i=1nxi2)12+(∑i=1nyi2)12\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}(x_i+y_i)^2\bigg)^{\frac{1}{2}}\leq\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2\bigg)^{\frac{1}{2}}+\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2\bigg)^{\frac{1}{2}}(i=1∑n​(xi​+yi​)2)21​≤(i=1∑n​xi2​)21​+(i=1∑n​yi2​)21​

解答​

  • (a) Use induction is easily to fininsh.
note

Cauchy-Schwarz inequality is (∑i=1nxiyi)2≤(∑i=1nxi2)(∑i=1nyi2)\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}x_iy_i\bigg)^2 \leq \bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2\bigg)\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2\bigg)(∑i=1n​xi​yi​)2≤(∑i=1n​xi2​)(∑i=1n​yi2​)

  • (b)

    ∑i=1n(xi+yi)2=∑i=1nxi2+∑i=1nyi2+2∑i=1nxiyi≤∑i=1nxi2+∑i=1nyi2+2(∑i=1nxi2)(∑i=1nyi2)=∑i=1nxi2+∑i=1nyi2\begin{align*} \sqrt{\sum^n_{i=1}(x_i+y_i)^2}&=\sqrt{\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2+\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2+2\sum^n_{i=1}x_iy_i}\\ &\leq\sqrt{\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2+\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2+2\sqrt{\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2\bigg)\bigg(\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2\bigg)}}\\ &=\sqrt{\sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2}+\sqrt{\sum^n_{i=1}y_i^2} \end{align*}i=1∑n​(xi​+yi​)2​​=i=1∑n​xi2​+i=1∑n​yi2​+2i=1∑n​xi​yi​​≤i=1∑n​xi2​+i=1∑n​yi2​+2(i=1∑n​xi2​)(i=1∑n​yi2​)​​=i=1∑n​xi2​​+i=1∑n​yi2​​​
