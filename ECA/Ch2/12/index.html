題目​

Prove the follwing properties for subsets AAA and BBB of a metric space:

  • (a) int⁡(int⁡(A))=int⁡(A)\operatorname{int}(\operatorname{int}(A)) = \operatorname{int}(A)int(int(A))=int(A)
  • (b) int⁡(A∪B)⊃int⁡(A)∪int⁡(B)\operatorname{int}(A\cup B)\supset\operatorname{int}(A)\cup\operatorname{int}(B)int(A∪B)⊃int(A)∪int(B)
  • (c) int⁡(A∩B)=int⁡(A)∩int⁡(B)\operatorname{int}(A\cap B)=\operatorname{int}(A)\cap\operatorname{int}(B)int(A∩B)=int(A)∩int(B) {{< /mq >}}

解答​

  • (a) Since int⁡(A)\operatorname{int}(A)int(A) is an open set, and we know if a set SSS is open, then int⁡(S)=S\operatorname{int}(S) = Sint(S)=S. Hence int⁡(int⁡(A))=int⁡(A).\operatorname{int}(\operatorname{int}(A)) = \operatorname{int}(A).int(int(A))=int(A).

  • (b) Wlog, choose x∈int⁡(A)x\in\operatorname{int}(A)x∈int(A), there exist a ϵ>0\epsilon > 0ϵ>0, s.t. D(x,ϵ)⊂A⊂A∪BD(x,\epsilon) \subset A \subset A\cup BD(x,ϵ)⊂A⊂A∪B, hence x∈int⁡(A∪B)x\in\operatorname{int}(A\cup B)x∈int(A∪B)

  • (c)

    ∀x∈int⁡(A∩B)  ⟺  there exist a ϵ>0,D(x,ϵ)⊂A∩B  ⟺  there exist a ϵ>0,D(x,ϵ)⊂A and D(x,ϵ)⊂B  ⟺  x∈int⁡(A) and x∈int⁡(B)  ⟺  x∈int⁡(A)∩int⁡(B)\begin{align*} &\forall x \in \operatorname{int}(A\cap B)\\ &\iff\text{there exist a }\epsilon > 0, D(x,\epsilon)\subset A\cap B\\ &\iff\text{there exist a }\epsilon > 0, D(x,\epsilon)\subset A\text{ and }D(x,\epsilon)\subset B\\ &\iff x\in\operatorname{int}(A)\text{ and }x\in\operatorname{int}(B)\\ &\iff x\in\operatorname{int}(A)\cap\operatorname{int}(B) \end{align*}​∀x∈int(A∩B)⟺there exist a ϵ>0,D(x,ϵ)⊂A∩B⟺there exist a ϵ>0,D(x,ϵ)⊂A and D(x,ϵ)⊂B⟺x∈int(A) and x∈int(B)⟺x∈int(A)∩int(B)​
