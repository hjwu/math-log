題目​

Let FnF_nFn​ denote the nnn-th fibonacci number. Let α=1+52.\alpha=\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}.α=21+5​​. Prove that

αn−1n5≤Fn.\frac{\alpha^{n-\frac{1}{n}}}{\sqrt{5}}\leq F_n.5​αn−n1​​≤Fn​.

解答​

首先我們知道幾件事

  1. Fn=αn−βn5,∀n>0, where β=1−52F_n = \frac{\alpha^n - \beta^n}{\sqrt{5}}, \forall n>0,\text{ where }\beta=\frac{1-\sqrt{5}}{2}Fn​=5​αn−βn​,∀n>0, where β=21−5​​
  2. βn≤α−n\beta^n \leq \alpha^{-n}βn≤α−n
  3. α2−α−1=0\alpha^2 - \alpha - 1 = 0α2−α−1=0

所以這題其實只要證下面這件事情就好。

αn−βn≥αn−1n\alpha^n-\beta^n\geq\alpha^{n-\frac{1}{n}}αn−βn≥αn−n1​

為了容易閱讀，先令 γ=1αn\gamma=\frac{1}{\sqrt[n]{\alpha}}γ=nα​1​。我們知道 α>1>γ\alpha>1>\gammaα>1>γ

∑i=0n−1(n−1i)αi≥∑i=0n−1γi  ⟹  (α+1)n−1≥1−γn1−γ  ⟹  (α+1)n−1(1−γ)≥1−γn  ⟹  (α2)n−1(1−1αn)≥1−1α  ⟹  α2n−2−α2n−2−1n≥1−1α  ⟹  α2n−α2n−1n≥α2−α=1  ⟹  αn−αn−1n≥α−n  ⟹  αn−α−n≥αn−1n\begin{aligned} &\quad &\sum\limits_{i=0}^{n-1} {n-1 \choose i}\alpha^{i} \geq \sum\limits_{i=0}^{n-1} \gamma^{i}\\ &\implies &(\alpha+1)^{n-1} \geq \frac{1-\gamma^n}{1-\gamma}\\ &\implies &(\alpha+1)^{n-1}(1-\gamma) \geq 1-\gamma^n\\ &\implies &(\alpha^2)^{n-1}(1-\frac{1}{\sqrt[n]{\alpha}}) \geq 1-\frac{1}{\alpha}\\ &\implies &\alpha^{2n-2}-\alpha^{2n-2-\frac{1}{n}}\geq 1-\frac{1}{\alpha}\\ &\implies &\alpha^{2n}-\alpha^{2n-\frac{1}{n}}\geq\alpha^2-\alpha =1\\ &\implies &\alpha^n-\alpha^{n-\frac{1}{n}} \geq \alpha^{-n}\\ &\implies &\alpha^n-\alpha^{-n} \geq \alpha^{n-\frac{1}{n}} \end{aligned}​⟹⟹⟹⟹⟹⟹⟹​i=0∑n−1​(in−1​)αi≥i=0∑n−1​γi(α+1)n−1≥1−γ1−γn​(α+1)n−1(1−γ)≥1−γn(α2)n−1(1−nα​1​)≥1−α1​α2n−2−α2n−2−n1​≥1−α1​α2n−α2n−n1​≥α2−α=1αn−αn−n1​≥α−nαn−α−n≥αn−n1​​

所以由上可得

αn−βn≥αn−α−n≥αn−1n\alpha^n-\beta^n \geq \alpha^n-\alpha^{-n} \geq \alpha^{n-\frac{1}{n}}αn−βn≥αn−α−n≥αn−n1​
