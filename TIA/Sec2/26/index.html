解答 (a)​

Let [0],[1],⋯ ,[p−1]{[0],[1],\cdots, [p-1]}[0],[1],⋯,[p−1] be the set of the congruence classes modulo ppp.

The number of occurrences of adadad in [0][0][0] is 2p−12p-12p−1, and in any other class, it is p−1p-1p−1.
