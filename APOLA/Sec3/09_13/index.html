題目​

If v1,⋯ ,vmv_1, \cdots, v_mv1​,⋯,vm​ are characteristic vectors associated with a1,⋯ ,ama_1, \cdots, a_ma1​,⋯,am​ which are distinct character roots of A∈Mn(F)\bm{A} \in \bm{M}_n(\mathcal{F})A∈Mn​(F), show that v1,⋯ ,vmv_1, \cdots, v_mv1​,⋯,vm​ are L.I.

Let the max number of L.I vectors are kkk. (Where k<mk < mk<m, otherwise. it's done.)

WLOG, let they are v1,⋯ ,vkv_1,\cdots,v_kv1​,⋯,vk​ and we have vk+1=b1v1+b2v2+⋯+bkvkv_{k+1} = b_{1}v_{1} + b_{2}v_{2} + \cdots + b_{k}v_{k}vk+1​=b1​v1​+b2​v2​+⋯+bk​vk​, where b1≠0b_1\neq 0b1​=0.

Since

ak+1(b1v1+b2v2+⋯+bkvk)=ak+1vk+1=Avk+1=A(b1v1+b2v2+⋯+bkvk)=b1Av1+b2Av2+⋯+bkAvk=∑i=1kaibivi\begin{align*} a_{k+1}(b_{1}v_{1} + b_{2}v_{2} + \cdots + b_{k}v_{k}) &=a_{k+1}v_{k+1}\\ &=\bm{A}v_{k+1}\\ &=\bm{A}(b_{1}v_{1} + b_{2}v_{2} + \cdots + b_{k}v_{k})\\ &=b_{1}\bm{A}v_{1} + b_{2}\bm{A}v_{2} + \cdots + b_{k}\bm{A}v_{k}\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^k a_{i}b_{i}v_{i} \end{align*}ak+1​(b1​v1​+b2​v2​+⋯+bk​vk​)​=ak+1​vk+1​=Avk+1​=A(b1​v1​+b2​v2​+⋯+bk​vk​)=b1​Av1​+b2​Av2​+⋯+bk​Avk​=i=1∑k​ai​bi​vi​​

then we have
