題目​

If at least one of A\bm{A}A or B\bm{B}B is invertible, prove that PAB(x)=PBA(x).P_{\bm{AB}}(x) = P_{\bm{BA}}(x).PAB​(x)=PBA​(x).

解答​

WLOG, let B\bm{B}B is invertible.

det⁡(xB−1I−A)=det⁡(B−1)det⁡(xI−BA)=det⁡(xI−AB)det⁡(B−1)\det(x\bm{B^{-1}I-A}) = \det(\bm{B^{-1}})\det(x\bm{I}-\bm{BA})=\det(x\bm{I}-\bm{AB})\det(\bm{B^{-1}})det(xB−1I−A)=det(B−1)det(xI−BA)=det(xI−AB)det(B−1)

then det⁡(xI−BA)=det⁡(xI−AB)\det(x\bm{I}-\bm{BA})=\det(x\bm{I}-\bm{AB})det(xI−BA)=det(xI−AB)
